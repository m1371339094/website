About Ability Care

Dedicated to enhancing lives through compassionate, professional disability support

Our Mission

At Ability Care, we believe everyone deserves to live with dignity, independence, and joy. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, person-centered disability support services that empower individuals to achieve their full potential and participate fully in their communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Ability Care began with a simple vision: to create a support service that truly understands and respects the unique needs of individuals with disabilities. What started as a small team of dedicated caregivers has grown into a comprehensive support organization serving over 500 clients across the region.

Our founder, inspired by personal family experiences with disability care, recognized the need for services that go beyond basic assistance. We focus on building genuine relationships, understanding individual goals, and creating customized support plans that enhance quality of life.

Our Team

Our team consists of highly trained, compassionate professionals including:

  • Certified Support Workers - Trained in person-centered care and specialized support techniques
  • Occupational Therapists - Helping maximize independence in daily activities
  • Physical Therapists - Supporting mobility and physical wellness
  • Speech Therapists - Assisting with communication needs
  • Care Coordinators - Ensuring seamless service delivery and family communication
  • Medical Professionals - Providing expert health monitoring and care

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing individualized, person-centered care plans
  • Maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and ethics
  • Continuous staff training and development
  • Building strong partnerships with families and healthcare providers
  • Advocating for the rights and inclusion of people with disabilities
  • Creating welcoming, accessible environments
