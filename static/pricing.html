Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the monthly fee?

All pricing includes support worker services, care coordination, emergency response, and basic supplies. Additional services like therapy sessions and transportation vary by plan.

Can I change my plan?

Yes! Your needs may change over time. You can upgrade, downgrade, or customize your plan at any time with 30 days notice.

Is there a contract?

We offer both month-to-month and annual contract options. Annual contracts receive a 10% discount.
